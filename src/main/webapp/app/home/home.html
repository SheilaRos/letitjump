Let it jump is a platform game of the Android operating system. It was created as an idea for a final project of CFGS of Development of multiplatform applications by five students of Stucom.
In this web page you will find sections like a question and answer forum, a world ranking with the best players, a page where you can find out the origin of the different characters and their role in history, as well as a section to manage your account.
Having said that, feel free to browse through the different sections.
